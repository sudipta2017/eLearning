Instructional Design & Course Development

Something I have learnt in these past years: Identifying the gaps, objectives, learner needs, and environmental constraints are amongst the primary things that drive a project to success because those are factors upon which an effective solution depends. I have worked on different training projects, some of them are showcased below.

Consulting For Mould Remediation

This curriculum consists of 5 modules and discusses standard operating procedures, regulations, health and safety measurements on handling mould remediation projects.

Wetland Assessment

This interactive module provides information on delineating wetlands in Canada, created to assist the environmental assessment team with their job.

Receipt Of Funds Records

It is an interactive training module to support employees with process updates. The expectation was to transfer knowledge, bring engagement to tedious content and increase the employee participation rate.

Audit And Assurance: Materiality

A highly interactive module developed using real-life simulation and gamification logic.

Cybersecurity

It is an interactive module outlining workplace best practices to protect data and information from being vulnerable.

Client Management

This module prepares managers to understand various aspects of client management and adopt some best practices with scenario-based examples.

Self-Guided Coaching For New Managers

It is a professional course developed for Schulich School of Business in a blended learning format.

Honeycomb App

Multiple training resources to introduce and implement a new system to the employees.

How To Avoid Tick Bites And Be Safe

It is an interactive course that provides information on how to be safe during the spring and summer months from having tick-borne diseases, which is a safety concern when employees work outdoors on areas reported with tick abundance.

Happy Halloween

It showcases microlearning on Halloween safety tips.

Not Myself Today

Example of continuous training snippet for encouraging employees' mental health awareness.
