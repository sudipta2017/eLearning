About the Project

Amidst fun and laughter, it’s that time of the year that can also bring serious safety risks when children are out after the sun is down, and there are lights and candles decorated in open areas with some scary figures. So, it’s a reminder for the adults to plan ahead, have fun while keeping their family and community safe.

This microlearning is created using 7taps.

For the best experience, please scan the QR code using your phone and enjoy.
