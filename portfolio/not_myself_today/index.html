About the Project

It is an initiative to build greater awareness and understanding of mental health among the workforce, reducing stigma and fostering safe and supportive work culture.

My Role

I have prepared an introduction video to introduce the program to the employees, gathering content provided by the Canadian Mental Health Association.

CMHA provides some practice activities and game ideas to play during team meetings. Some of them are in-person, which becomes useless for them working from home. I converted these games into online activities.

Tools used: Vyond Animation, Articulate Storyline
