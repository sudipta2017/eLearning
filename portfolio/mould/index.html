Project Background

Initially, a 2-day seminar, led by experts before the pandemic to empower employees with the standard procedures, methods, health and safety guidelines, tips and tricks from their long-time experience, converted into webinar sessions in the new-normal environment. The objective remains same: prepare the staff to effectively do their job.

The SMEs felt that webinars were not as effective because they were probably not getting the total attention of the attendees as they may be passively listening to them (and maybe doing other tasks at side). The concern was that people were making mistakes at work.

My Role

I suggested having a blended approach:

  1. A self-paced online course with multiple modules to cover the information, case studies, knowledge checks at intervals to encourage their cognitive involvement and a final assessment at the end of the course
  2. A monthly one-hour virtual session – “Ask The Experts” with the SMEs, where employees (existing and new) can register and SMEs will answer their queries.

I collaborated with the SMEs to design storyboards, interviewed them to gather information, supported them in recording their voice that I used within the modules, created assessment templates, created multimedia, developed the course and launched it.

Training resources created:

  1. A course consisting of 5 online modules
  2. Final Assessment
  3. Job-aids to download and use as reference when needed

Tools used: Articulate Rise, Vyond animation, Photoshop, Camtasia

Result

Employees were able to take responsibility for their training and validate their knowledge through assessments. After they took the online course, employees were proactive and specific with queries during the virtual session.

SMEs spent time only answering queries and sharing valuable experiences (and not repeating information) during live sessions. It helped free up their time for other tasks. The error rate was significantly reduced.
